Wellness Library

Explore our curated collection of exercises, guided meditations, and educational videos. Select a topic below to begin your healing practice.

Anxiety Relief

Quick Techniques:

  • 4-7-8 Breathing: Inhale for 4s, hold for 7s, exhale for 8s.
  • Grounding: Feel your feet on the floor and name 3 things you see.
🎧 Guided Breathing for Anxiety (5 min)
Coping with Depression

Daily Steps:

  • Behavioral Activation: Do one small task, even if you don't feel like it.
  • Sunlight: 15 minutes of morning light to boost serotonin.
Managing Stress
  • Brain Dump: Write down everything worrying you to clear your mind.
  • Progressive Muscle Relaxation: Tense and relax each muscle group.
🎧 10-Minute Stress Melt Meditation
ADHD & Focus Strategies
  • Pomodoro Technique: Work for 25 mins, break for 5 mins.
  • Body Doubling: Work alongside someone else to stay on task.
Anger Management
  • The Pause Button: Walk away from the situation for 20 minutes before responding.
  • Cooling Thoughts: Repeat "This is not worth my peace" internally.
Building Self-Esteem
  • Mirror Work: Look in the mirror and say 3 kind things to yourself.
  • Success Journal: Write down 5 things you did well today, no matter how small.
Navigating Grief
  • Honor the Emotion: Allow yourself to cry; it releases stress hormones.
  • Memory Box: Create a physical space for memories of your loved one.
Better Sleep (Insomnia)
  • 4-7-8 Breathing: Helps induce sleepiness.
  • Sleep Stories: Distract the mind from racing thoughts.
🎧 Deep Sleep Guided Relaxation
Autism & Sensory Support
  • Stimming: Allow yourself to move (flap, rock) to regulate emotions.
  • Safe Space: Retreat to a quiet, dark room for 15 minutes.
🎧 Sensory Regulation Audio
